Savings Fund Guidelines

Contribution:

  • Share per member is Php only
  • Maximum of 6 shares per member only.
  • Extension members (father/mother/brother/sister/boyfriend/girlfriend) are allowed to join with a maximum of 4 shares only.
  • Shares are to be paid within 3 working days after each payday (every 15th and end of the month).
  • A penalty of Php per share will be imposed for those members who failed to pay on the due date.

Loan:

  • Members are allowed to loan a maximum of Php 5,000 on a single loan depending on the total amount saved. Amount beyond Php 5,000 is subject for approval.
  • Members can re-loan, provided he/she has no outstanding loans or has settled his/her remaining balance of the previous loan. Re-loan with existing unsettled loan is subject for approval.
  • Members are charged a monthly interest of 3% of the total loan amount.
  • Non-members are also allowed to loan a maximum of Php 2,000.00, but he/she must be endorsed by a member. Members will be responsible to collect the payables.
    • \
  • Non-members are charged a monthly interest of 5% of the total loan amount.
  • All loans are to be paid in 4 consecutive paydays after the loan release date.
  • A penalty of 3% for members and 5% for non-members shall be imposed monthly on loans not paid after due date. Due date is 2 months after the loan release date (i.e. Loan release date is January 15, 2014, due date will be March 15, 2014).

Computation:

  • Interest are computed by Total Interest - Patronage divided by Total Shares (interest = total_interest - patronage / total_shares)
  • Patronage is computed by number of members(1% per member) and a maximum of % or which ever is lower.

  • Patronage share will be given to the collector for his/her service, and shall be 1% per member or 12%, whichever is lower.
  • The percent of patronage share shall be taken out from the total interest earned. (i.e Total number of persons who joined the Savings Fund: 8
  • Hence, 8% of the interest earned shall be given to the collector for his/her service.)
  • 100% percent of the Net income will be divided among all members based on their number of shares. Net income is Gross interest earned less patronage share.
  • Higher number of shares equals higher interest share.
